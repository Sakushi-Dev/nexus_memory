Layer V · the diary

A long-term narrative that stays bounded — forever.

The diary compresses raw dialogue into a time-pyramid and keeps it bounded, without the module ever calling an LLM. It just enqueues jobs; your host drains them on any model.

The time-pyramid

Fine detail near the present, coarse epochs as you go back — bounded by a ring.

L0 · episodic_turns
Raw turns
every user / assistant message
▼ rolling, every 3 interactions
L1 · diary_days

▼ folded, 7 days per section
L2 · persistent_sections
Epoch summaries

The handoff outbox

Provider-agnostic by construction — Nexus owns the prompt, you own the model.

1
ingest

A summary comes due → Nexus enqueues a job (prompt + context) into the outbox. No LLM is called.

2
pending_summaries()

Your host pulls the due jobs — synchronously, async, batched, or with a human in the loop.

3
run on your model

Execute the job's prompt on any provider — a cheap “aux” model, a local SLM, whatever you like.

4
submit_summary()

Hand the text back; Nexus folds it into the pyramid. Idempotent — re-submitting is a safe no-op.
