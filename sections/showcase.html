Layer V · the diary

A long-term narrative that stays bounded — forever.

The diary compresses raw dialogue into a bounded, session-wise narrative, without the module ever calling an LLM. It just enqueues jobs; your host drains them on any model.

The session narrative

A rolling per-session entry on top, folded into one growing persistent summary.

L0 · episodic_turns
Raw turns
every user / assistant message
▼ rolling, over the session window
L1 · session entry
One entry per session
an LLM narrative, rolled in place
▼ folded, every few sessions
L2 · persistent_summary
One growing summary
a single cross-session prose, capped & bounded

The handoff outbox

Provider-agnostic by construction — Nexus owns the prompt, you own the model.

1
ingest

A summary comes due → Nexus enqueues a job (prompt + context) into the outbox. No LLM is called.

2
pending_summaries()

Your host pulls the due jobs — synchronously, async, batched, or with a human in the loop.

3
run on your model

Execute the job's prompt on any provider — a cheap “aux” model, a local SLM, whatever you like.

4
submit_summary()

Hand the text back; Nexus rolls it into the session entry. Idempotent — re-submitting is a safe no-op.
